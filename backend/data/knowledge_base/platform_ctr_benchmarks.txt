PLATFORM CTR BENCHMARKS — DIGITAL ADVERTISING
Updated: 2025 (sources: WordStream 2025, LocaliQ 2025, Focus Digital 2026, Smart Insights 2024)

---

GOOGLE SEARCH ADS
- Average CTR (all industries, 2025): 6.66% (WordStream); cross-industry range 3.52%–6.11%
- High-performing campaigns: 8%–12%
- Below average: <2%
- Industry leaders: Legal (5.97%, +13% YoY), Health & Wellness (2.43%), Automotive (1.65%, +16% YoY)
- Fastest growing: Sports & Recreation (+40% YoY), Education (+40% YoY)
- Food & Beverage: 0.93% (display-heavy mix drags average)
- Note: 52% of industries saw CTR decline in 2025 despite rising overall average

GOOGLE DISPLAY ADS
- Average CTR: 0.35%–0.46%
- High-performing (rich media): 1%+
- Display CTR is ~10x lower than Search
- Remarketing campaigns: 0.7%–1.2%

META / FACEBOOK ADS
- Average CTR (traffic campaigns, all industries, 2025): 1.71% (up from 1.57% in 2024)
- Average CTR (leads campaigns, 2025): 2.59%
- Cross-industry average: 1.44%–1.71%
- Good performance threshold: >1.49% (top half of competitors)
- Underperformance signal: <0.72%
- Every industry improved YoY in 2025: gains ranging +5.48% to +25.45%
- Facebook video ads (traffic objective): 1.57% avg across 23 industries
- Median CTR 2025: 2.19% (signals strong visual format engagement)

INSTAGRAM ADS
- Feed ads: 0.22%–0.88% CTR
- Feed video ads: ~1.2% (Retail and Legal sectors especially strong)
- Stories ads: 0.33%–0.54%
- Reels: 1.2%–2.5% (highest reach + engagement format, algorithm-prioritized)
- Shopping ads: 0.8%–1.8%
- Top-performing creative can reach 3%+

LINKEDIN ADS
- Average CTR (2025): 0.52% (reflects long B2B consideration cycles)
- Sponsored content: 0.35%–0.50%
- Message ads (InMail): 15%–25% open rate, 3%–5% CTR
- Lead gen forms: 10%–13% conversion rate after click
- CPC is 5x–10x higher than Facebook — justified by professional audience quality

TIKTOK ADS
- Average CTR (2025): 0.61%–0.84% (mid-range benchmark)
- In-feed ads: 0.5%–1.5%
- High-performing creative: 1.5%–3% (TikTok-native style, trending sounds)
- TopView ads (premium placement): 12%–16% CTR
- TikTok-first ads capture attention for 74% of viewers and drive 3.3× more action vs repurposed content
- Creative burnout: TikTok ads lose effectiveness within 1–2 weeks; refresh frequently

TWITTER / X ADS
- Average CTR (2025): 0.86% (closely matches Facebook's 0.90%)
- Promoted tweets: 1%–3% engagement rate
- Website cards: 0.5%–1.2%

YOUTUBE ADS
- Average CTR (2025–2026): 0.65% (stable)
- Skippable in-stream: 0.3%–0.5%
- Non-skippable: 0.4%–0.8%
- Bumper ads (6s): awareness-focused; CTR less relevant than view-through
- Note: YouTube CTR is lower but view-through conversions and brand recall are high

---

INTERPRETATION GUIDE

A CTR above platform average indicates strong creative-audience fit.
A CTR below 50% of platform average suggests poor targeting, weak creative, or audience mismatch.
CTR alone does not indicate profitability — always evaluate alongside conversion rate and CPA.
