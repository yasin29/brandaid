PLATFORM CTR BENCHMARKS — DIGITAL ADVERTISING

Source: Industry aggregated benchmarks (WordStream, HubSpot, Statista, Meta Business Insights, Google Ads benchmarks 2023–2024)

---

GOOGLE SEARCH ADS
- Average CTR (all industries): 3.17%
- High-performing campaigns: 5%–8%
- Below average: <1.5%
- Best industries: Legal (4.35%), Dating (6.05%), Finance (2.65%)
- Worst industries: Technology (1.84%), B2B (2.41%)

GOOGLE DISPLAY ADS
- Average CTR: 0.35%–0.46%
- High-performing: 1%+
- Display CTR is typically 10x lower than Search
- Remarketing campaigns: 0.7%–1.2%

META / FACEBOOK ADS
- Average CTR (all formats): 0.9%–1.5%
- Feed ads: 0.9%
- Stories ads: 0.6%–1.2%
- High-performing campaigns: 2%–5%
- Retail/e-commerce average: 1.59%
- Finance: 0.56%
- Technology: 1.04%

INSTAGRAM ADS
- Average CTR: 0.68%–1.2%
- Stories: 0.5%–1.0%
- Reels: 1.2%–2.5% (higher engagement format)
- Shopping ads: 0.8%–1.8%
- Strong visual campaigns can reach 3%+

LINKEDIN ADS
- Average CTR: 0.35%–0.65%
- Sponsored content: 0.35%–0.50%
- Message ads (InMail): 15%–25% open rate, 3%–5% CTR
- Lead gen forms: 10%–13% conversion rate after click
- B2B targeting performs significantly better than B2C on LinkedIn

TIKTOK ADS
- Average CTR: 0.5%–1.5%
- In-feed ads: 0.5%–1.8%
- TopView ads: 12%–16% CTR (premium placement)
- Branded hashtag challenges: high engagement but not direct CTR
- Gen Z and Millennial audiences: 1.5%–3% on well-produced creative

TWITTER / X ADS
- Average CTR: 0.4%–0.9%
- Promoted tweets: 1%–3% engagement rate
- Website cards: 0.5%–1.2%

YOUTUBE ADS
- Average CTR (skippable in-stream): 0.3%–0.5%
- Non-skippable: 0.4%–0.8%
- Bumper ads (6s): awareness-focused, CTR less relevant
- Reminder: YouTube CTR is lower but view-through conversions are high

---

INTERPRETATION GUIDE

A CTR above platform average indicates strong creative-audience fit.
A CTR below 50% of platform average suggests poor targeting, weak creative, or audience mismatch.
CTR alone does not indicate profitability — always consider conversion rate and CPA alongside CTR.
