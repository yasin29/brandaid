ROAS AND CONVERSION RATE BENCHMARKS — DIGITAL ADVERTISING

Source: Industry aggregated benchmarks (Nielsen, WordStream, HubSpot, Shopify, Google, Meta 2023–2024)

---

RETURN ON AD SPEND (ROAS) — BY INDUSTRY

ROAS = Revenue generated / Ad spend
A ROAS of 4x means $4 earned for every $1 spent.

Minimum viable ROAS (break-even, assuming 25% margin): 4x
Strong ROAS: 6x–10x
Exceptional ROAS: 10x+

By Industry:
- E-commerce / Retail: 4x–8x average, 10x+ top performers
- Fashion / Apparel: 3x–6x
- Health & Wellness: 5x–9x
- Software / SaaS: 3x–5x (higher LTV offsets lower ROAS)
- Finance / Insurance: 2x–4x (high CPA but high LTV)
- Food & Beverage: 4x–7x
- Education: 3x–6x
- Travel: 4x–8x (highly seasonal)
- Beauty / Cosmetics: 5x–10x
- B2B Services: 2x–4x (long sales cycle)

By Platform:
- Google Search: 5x–10x (high intent)
- Google Shopping: 4x–8x
- Meta/Facebook: 3x–6x
- Instagram: 3x–7x
- TikTok: 2x–5x (brand awareness heavy)
- LinkedIn: 1.5x–3x (high CPC, but B2B LTV)
- YouTube: 3x–6x (view-through attribution)

---

CONVERSION RATES — BY INDUSTRY AND FUNNEL STAGE

Landing page conversion rates (visit → lead):
- E-commerce: 2.5%–4%
- SaaS / Software: 3%–7%
- Finance: 5%–11%
- Healthcare: 3%–6%
- Legal: 6%–12%
- Real Estate: 2%–4%
- Travel: 2%–4%

E-commerce checkout conversion (add to cart → purchase):
- Average: 2.5%–3.5%
- High-performing: 5%+
- Mobile typically 30%–40% lower than desktop

Lead-to-customer conversion (B2B):
- Average: 2%–5%
- With strong nurture: 10%–15%

---

COST PER ACQUISITION (CPA) — BY PLATFORM

Google Search Ads average CPA (all industries): $48
Meta/Facebook average CPA: $18
Instagram average CPA: $22
LinkedIn average CPA: $98 (B2B, high LTV justified)
TikTok average CPA: $14–$25
YouTube average CPA: $38

---

BUDGET EFFICIENCY SIGNALS

Low budget (<$500/month): Insufficient data collection, high CPA, use narrow targeting
Mid budget ($500–$5,000/month): Can test 2–3 audiences, some optimization possible
High budget ($5,000–$50,000/month): A/B testing, lookalike audiences, full funnel strategy
Enterprise ($50k+/month): Algorithmic optimization, cross-platform attribution, custom audiences

---

CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVE IMPACT ON METRICS

Awareness campaigns: Optimize for impressions, reach, CPM — not CTR or ROAS
Consideration campaigns: Optimize for CTR, CPC, video views
Conversion campaigns: Optimize for ROAS, CPA, conversion rate
Retargeting campaigns: Typically 2x–5x better ROAS than prospecting
