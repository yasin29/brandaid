ROAS AND CONVERSION RATE BENCHMARKS — DIGITAL ADVERTISING
Updated: 2025 (sources: WordStream 2025, Triple Whale 2025, First Page Sage 2025, Lebesgue 2026, Trendtrack 2025)

---

RETURN ON AD SPEND (ROAS) — BY PLATFORM (2025)

ROAS = Revenue generated / Ad spend. A ROAS of 4× means $4 earned per $1 spent.

Platform benchmarks (2025 medians):
- Google Search: 5.17× (highest — driven by high-intent search behavior; declined ~10% YoY to 3.68× overall)
- Google Shopping: 4.0×–8.0×
- Meta/Facebook: 2.19× avg across all industries (Facebook avg 2025)
- Instagram: 2.5×–5.0×
- TikTok: 2.21× (growing but weaker conversion tracking than Meta)
- LinkedIn: 1.5×–3.0× (high CPC offset by B2B LTV)
- YouTube: 3.0×–6.0× (view-through attribution critical)

Key 2025 ROAS trends:
- Average eCommerce ROAS dropped to 2.87× in 2025 (from ~3.2× in 2024) — industry-wide efficiency decline
- Median brand ROAS across all channels in 2024: 2.04×
- ROAS declined across 13 of 14 industries in 2025 on Google (rising CPCs, more competition)
- TikTok: lower CPM but weaker ROAS vs Meta; better for net-new customer acquisition (10% new-to-brand delta vs Meta's 38%)

RETURN ON AD SPEND (ROAS) — BY INDUSTRY

Minimum viable ROAS (break-even at 25% margin): 4×
Good ROAS: 4×–6×
Strong ROAS: 6×–10×
Exceptional: 10×+

By Industry (realistic 2025 ranges):
- E-commerce / Retail: 2.87×–6.0× (median 2.87×; top performers 8×–12×)
- Fashion / Apparel: 3.0×–6.0×
- Health & Wellness / Beauty: 4.0×–8.0× (strong UGC-driven performance)
- Beauty / Cosmetics: 4.0×–10.0×
- Software / SaaS: 2.0×–5.0× (lower ROAS acceptable due to high LTV)
- Finance / Insurance: 2.0×–4.0× (high CPA, justified by LTV)
- Food & Beverage: 3.0×–6.0×
- Education: 2.5×–5.0×
- Travel: 3.0×–7.0× (highly seasonal; ROAS swings dramatically by quarter)
- B2B Services: 1.5×–3.5× (long sales cycle; attribute to pipeline, not direct ROAS)
- Real Estate: 2.0×–5.0×

CONVERSION RATES — BY INDUSTRY AND FUNNEL STAGE (2025)

Ad click-to-conversion rates (2025):
- TikTok avg: 2.01% (up from 1.92% in 2024)
- Google Search avg: 3.5%–6% (high-intent; varies by industry)
- Meta avg: 1.5%–3% (depends heavily on funnel stage)

Landing page conversion rates (click → lead/purchase):
- E-commerce: 2.5%–4%
- SaaS / Software: 3%–7%
- Finance: 5%–11%
- Healthcare: 3%–6%
- Legal: 6%–12%
- Real Estate: 5.45% (2025 cold traffic) — higher than expected due to intent
- Travel: 1.04% (2025 cold traffic) — lower due to high consideration cycle

E-commerce checkout conversion (add to cart → purchase):
- Average: 2.5%–3.5%
- High-performing: 5%+
- Mobile typically 30%–40% lower than desktop

Lead-to-customer conversion (B2B):
- Average: 2%–5%
- With strong nurture sequences: 10%–15%

COST PER ACQUISITION (CPA) — BY PLATFORM (2025)

TikTok avg CPA: $32.74 (CPM: $13.26; approaching Meta parity)
Meta/Facebook avg CPA: $18–$25 (CPM: $14.19 median)
Google Search avg CPA: $40–$65 (varies by industry)
Instagram avg CPA: $22–$35
LinkedIn avg CPA: $75–$130 (B2B; high LTV justified)
YouTube avg CPA: $30–$50

TikTok CPC benchmark (2025): Good CPC < $0.25 for efficient industries; range $0.05–$1.00

BUDGET EFFICIENCY SIGNALS

Low budget (<$500/month): High CPA, insufficient conversion data; narrow targeting essential
Mid budget ($500–$5,000/month): Can test 2–3 audiences; some optimization possible
High budget ($5,000–$50,000/month): Full A/B testing, lookalike audiences, full funnel strategy
Enterprise ($50k+/month): Algorithmic optimization, cross-platform attribution, custom audiences

Meta learning phase minimum: $5/day × 7 days to exit (or 50 conversions in 7 days)

---

CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVE IMPACT ON METRICS

Awareness: Optimize for impressions, reach, CPM — CTR and ROAS less relevant
Consideration: Optimize for CTR, CPC, video views, engagement rate
Conversion: Optimize for ROAS, CPA, conversion rate
Retargeting: Typically 2×–5× better ROAS than cold prospecting
Retargeting + UGC creative: Top-performing combination in 2024–2025 DTC data
