PLATFORM-SPECIFIC ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES
Updated: 2025 (sources: Meta Business 2025, Billo.app 2025, DeanLong.io 2025, TikTok for Business 2025, LeadsBridge 2026, Motion App 2025)

---

META / FACEBOOK ADS

Creative (2025 data):
- 4:5 vertical format outperforms 1:1 square in Feed placements by up to 15%
- Split-screen formats improve recall by up to 12% (better memory retention)
- Static images still drive 60%–70% of conversions on Meta — don't over-index on video
- First 3 seconds are critical for video — hook immediately or lose viewer
- Optimal video length: 15–30 seconds for feed; under 15s for stories
- Captions: 85% of Facebook videos watched without sound — always add captions
- 70%–80% of Meta ad performance stems from creative quality — not budget or targeting
- Creative volume: test 15–25 genuinely different ads per campaign (mix formats, copy lengths, angles)
- Interviews and drama hooks outperform playful formats for conversion objectives
- Text overlay: Keep under 20% of image area for full delivery

Targeting:
- Lookalike audiences (1%–2% similarity): Most cost-efficient for scaling
- Detailed interest targeting: Good for top-of-funnel awareness
- Retargeting website visitors: Typically 2×–5× better ROAS than cold audiences
- Avoid over-narrowing: Keep audience above 500k for algorithm to optimize
- Advantage+ Audiences (Meta's AI targeting): Outperforming manual targeting in most cases in 2025

Copy:
- Optimal headline: 5 words or fewer (under 125 characters total copy)
- Body text: First line must hook without "See More" expansion
- Test one-sentence copy against blog-post-length; medium-length (3–4 sentences) often performs worst
- Sell benefits, not features; focus on the transformation, not the product
- Strong CTA: "Shop Now" and "Learn More" consistently outperform generic CTAs

Budget:
- Learning phase: Min $5/day × 7 days (or 50 conversions in 7 days) to exit
- Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO): Recommended for 3+ ad sets
- Spend at least $10–$20/day per ad set for meaningful data

INSTAGRAM ADS

Creative:
- Reels: Highest reach potential in 2025 — algorithm-prioritized; 1.2% avg CTR for video
- Stories: Swipe-up / link sticker for direct conversion; keep under 15s; CTR 0.33%–0.54%
- Feed: High-quality visuals essential — lower tolerance for lo-fi than TikTok
- Carousels: Higher engagement (+3×) for consideration-stage campaigns; good for multi-product
- Feed video avg CTR ~1.2% (Retail and Legal strongest sectors)
- Influencer-style creative outperforms traditional studio ads 2×–3×
- UGC-style: 4× higher CTR than polished studio content

Audience:
- Ages 18–34 most active and most purchase-intent responsive
- 60% of consumers discover products through Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok combined
- Strong for fashion, beauty, lifestyle, food, fitness — weaker for B2B

GOOGLE SEARCH ADS

Structure:
- 3–5 headlines, 2 descriptions per ad for Responsive Search Ads (RSA)
- Include primary keyword in Headline 1
- Ad extensions (Sitelinks, Callouts, Structured Snippets): Increase CTR 10%–20%
- Headline 3: Use for promotions or urgency CTAs

Targeting:
- Exact match: Highest intent, lowest volume — best for conversion campaigns
- Phrase match: Best balance of intent and volume
- Broad match: Only effective with Smart Bidding; risky without conversion data
- Negative keywords: Critical — add from day 1 to eliminate irrelevant spend

Bidding:
- Target CPA or Target ROAS: Effective after 30+ conversions in 30 days
- Manual CPC: Better for new campaigns without conversion history
- 2025 performance: Search ROAS 5.17× avg (still highest of any platform)

TIKTOK ADS

Creative (2025 key rules):
- "Don't make ads, make TikToks" — native content style drives 3.3× more action than repurposed ads
- 74% of viewers say TikTok-first ads capture their attention vs repurposed content
- First 2–3 seconds: Must grab attention or swipe happens immediately
- Duration: 9–15 seconds optimal; 30s max for most objectives
- Sound-on by default: Use trending audio; 74%+ of TikTok views have sound on
- Text overlays, voiceovers, green screen, duets, stitches: Use native features to feel organic
- Subtitles: Include for accessibility and the minority who watch muted
- Creative fatigue: TikTok ads burn out within 1–2 weeks — refresh creative constantly

Performance data:
- Avg CTR: 0.61%–0.84% (2025)
- Avg CVR: 2.01%; avg CPA: $32.74; avg CPM: $13.26
- TikTok generates more net-new customers than Meta (10% new-to-brand rate vs Meta's 38%)
- B2C products perform strongly; B2B effectiveness very limited on TikTok

Audience:
- Gen Z (16–24): Dominant, highest engagement; 72% of Gen Z use TikTok
- Millennials (25–34): Fast-growing segment on platform
- Interest targeting less refined than Meta — creator, hashtag, and behavioral targeting preferred

LINKEDIN ADS

Creative:
- Thought leadership content: Highest engagement for B2B decision-makers
- Single-image ads: Simple, professional design — avoid busy or consumer-style creative
- Video: 15–30 seconds for brand awareness; lead gen performs better as image + form
- Document ads: Very high engagement for whitepapers and reports
- Lead Gen Forms (native): 10%–13% conversion rate — significantly outperform external landing pages

Targeting:
- Job title: Most precise but most expensive
- Job function + seniority: Better volume with reasonable precision
- Company size + industry: Strong for ABM (account-based marketing)
- Avoid stacking too many targeting layers — keep audience above 50k

Expectations:
- LinkedIn CPC: 5×–10× higher than Facebook — budget accordingly
- Justified by professional audience quality and B2B LTV
- Best for top-of-funnel awareness and mid-funnel lead generation in B2B
- 92% of B2B marketers say case studies are most persuasive format — use in LinkedIn creative

---

YOUTUBE ADS

Creative:
- Skippable in-stream: First 5 seconds must hook — these are non-skippable
- Non-skippable (15s): Every second must earn viewer attention
- Optimal arc (even in 15–30s): Problem → Solution → CTA
- Add captions — 60% of views on mobile, often without sound

Targeting:
- Custom intent audiences (based on Google Search behavior): Highest purchase intent on YouTube
- YouTube channel targeting: Place ads alongside competitor or complementary content
- Affinity audiences: Better for awareness than conversion
- Avg CTR: 0.65% (stable 2025–2026)

GENERAL BEST PRACTICES ACROSS ALL PLATFORMS (2025)

1. Creative is the primary lever: 56%–80% of performance variation is driven by creative quality
2. Test 3–5 creative variants in the first week; pause underperformers at 48–72 hours
3. Refresh creative every 2–3 weeks (TikTok); 3–4 weeks (Meta/Instagram); 4–6 weeks (YouTube/LinkedIn)
4. UGC-style consistently outperforms polished studio content 3–5× across ROAS, CVR, CPM
5. Mobile-first: 70%–80% of social ad consumption is on mobile — design for small screens first
6. Landing page alignment: Ad message must match landing page — mismatch kills conversion rate
7. Funnel stage alignment: Awareness creative ≠ Conversion creative; mismatching kills efficiency
8. Frequency cap: Keep under 3–4×/week per user to avoid negative brand sentiment
9. Sound strategy: Facebook/Instagram = assume muted; TikTok/YouTube = assume sound-on
10. AI targeting: Meta's Advantage+ and Google's Performance Max increasingly outperform manual in 2025
