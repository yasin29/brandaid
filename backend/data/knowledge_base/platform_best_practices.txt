PLATFORM-SPECIFIC ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES

Source: Meta Business Help Center, Google Ads guidelines, TikTok for Business, LinkedIn Marketing Solutions, 2023–2024

---

META / FACEBOOK ADS

Creative:
- Video ads: First 3 seconds are critical — hook immediately or lose viewer
- Optimal video length: 15–30 seconds for feed; under 15s for stories
- Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5) format outperforms landscape in feed by 20%–30%
- Text overlay: Keep under 20% of image area for full reach (Meta policy)
- Captions: 85% of Facebook videos watched without sound — always add captions

Targeting:
- Lookalike audiences (1%–2% similarity): Most cost-efficient for scaling
- Detailed interest targeting: Works well for top-of-funnel awareness
- Retargeting website visitors: Typically 2x–5x better ROAS than cold audiences
- Avoid over-narrowing: Keep audience above 500k for algorithm to optimize

Copy:
- Optimal headline: 5 words or fewer
- Body text: First line must hook without "See More" expansion needed
- Strong CTA: "Shop Now" and "Learn More" consistently outperform generic CTAs
- Emojis: Can increase engagement by 25%–40% with right audience

Budget:
- Learning phase: Min $5/day for 7 days to exit learning phase
- Campaign budget optimization (CBO): Recommended for 3+ ad sets
- Spend at least $10–$20 per day per ad set for meaningful data

---

INSTAGRAM ADS

Creative:
- Reels: Highest reach potential in 2024 — prioritized by algorithm
- Stories: Swipe-up/link sticker for direct conversion; keep under 15s
- Feed: High-quality visuals essential — lower tolerance for lo-fi than TikTok
- Carousels: Higher engagement (+3x) for consideration-stage campaigns

Audience:
- Ages 18–34 most active and most responsive
- Influencer-style creative outperforms traditional ads by 2x–3x
- UGC (user-generated content) style: 4x higher CTR than polished studio content

---

GOOGLE SEARCH ADS

Structure:
- 3–5 headlines, 2 descriptions per ad for Responsive Search Ads (RSA)
- Include primary keyword in headline 1
- Use Ad Extensions: Sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets increase CTR 10%–20%
- Headline 3: Use for promotions or CTAs

Targeting:
- Exact match: Highest intent, lowest volume
- Phrase match: Best balance of intent and volume
- Broad match: Requires Smart Bidding to be effective; risky without it
- Negative keywords: Critical — add from day 1 to avoid irrelevant spend

Bidding:
- Target CPA or Target ROAS bidding: Effective after 30+ conversions in 30 days
- Manual CPC: Better for low-volume or new campaigns without conversion data
- Enhanced CPC: Good middle ground

---

TIKTOK ADS

Creative:
- "Don't make ads, make TikToks" — native content style dramatically outperforms
- First 2–3 seconds: Must grab attention or video is swiped
- Trending sounds/music: 3x higher engagement rate
- Duration: 9–15 seconds optimal; 30s max for most objectives
- Text on screen: Keep captions and hooks visible for 3+ seconds
- Subtitles: Required for accessibility and sound-off viewers

Audience:
- Gen Z (16–24): Dominant, highest engagement
- Millennials (25–34): Fast-growing segment
- B2C products perform strongly; B2B is limited
- Interest targeting less refined than Meta — creator/hashtag targeting preferred

---

LINKEDIN ADS

Creative:
- Thought leadership content: Highest engagement for B2B
- Single-image ads: Simple, professional — avoid busy designs
- Video: 15–30 seconds for brand awareness; lead gen performs better as image
- Document ads: Very high engagement for whitepapers and reports

Targeting:
- Job title targeting: Most precise but most expensive
- Job function + seniority: Better volume with reasonable precision
- Company size + industry: Strong for ABM (account-based marketing)
- Avoid stacking too many layers — keep audience above 50k

Expectations:
- LinkedIn CPC is 5x–10x higher than Facebook — budget accordingly
- Justified by higher intent for B2B and professional audiences
- Best for top-of-funnel awareness and mid-funnel lead generation in B2B

---

YOUTUBE ADS

Creative:
- Skippable in-stream: First 5 seconds must hook — these are non-skippable
- Non-skippable (15s): Every second must earn viewer attention
- Optimal storytelling arc: Problem → Solution → CTA (even in 15–30s)
- Add captions — 60% of views on mobile, often without sound

Targeting:
- Custom intent audiences (based on Google Search behavior): High purchase intent
- YouTube channel targeting: Place ads alongside competitor or complementary content
- Affinity audiences: Better for awareness than conversion

---

GENERAL BEST PRACTICES ACROSS ALL PLATFORMS

1. Test creative early: Run 3–5 variants in first week, pause underperformers
2. Refresh creative every 3–4 weeks to combat ad fatigue
3. Align creative with funnel stage: Awareness ≠ Conversion creative
4. Mobile-first design: 70%–80% of social ad consumption is on mobile
5. Landing page alignment: Ad message must match landing page — mismatch kills conversion
6. Campaign objectives: Always match objective to business goal; clicks ≠ conversions
7. Frequency cap: Keep frequency under 3–4x/week to avoid negative brand sentiment
